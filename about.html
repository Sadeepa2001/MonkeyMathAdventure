About the Game

Welcome to Monkey Math Adventure! This game is designed to test and enhance your math skills while having fun with our monkey character.

How to Play:

  • Select a difficulty level: Easy, Medium, or Hard.
  • Answer the math questions that appear on the screen.
  • Earn points for each correct answer and try to reach the highest score!
  • Keep an eye on the timer and your remaining lives.
  • Have fun and improve your math skills!
